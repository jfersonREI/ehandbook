Warning status

Important Announcement - Electronic Handbook Knowledge base (Help) is not accessible. Technical teams are aware of the issue and working towards to resolve this. Reminder: Recipients expending 1750,000 or more in federal awards during their fiscal years must have audits completed in accordance with 45 CFR Part 75, Subpart F and submit the related audit reports to the Federal Audit Clearinghouse within the earlier of 30 days after receipt from the auditors or nine months after the end of the audit periods. On Thursday, April 21, 2022, from 8:00 PM to 9:00 PM EST, HRSA OIT will perform maintenance activities to reconfigure External Production Tableau servers. During this maintenance window (8:00 PM to 9:00 PM, April 21), HRSA EHBs Dashboard Service will be unavailable ONLY for external grantees to access through HRSA EHBs. Internal Tableau Access will not be impacted. As of Oct. 1, 2020, HRSA began using the new standard HHS Page 1 format for all HRSA Notices of Award (NoA). All HHS agencies will use the new format to provide universal, streamlined data. Please visit https://www.hrsa.gov/grants/news-announcements.html for more information. On Thursday, October 27, 2022, from 7:00 PM to 9:00 PM EDT, HRSA Office of Information Technology (OIT) will be performing maintenance on the EHBs system. Users may experience intermittent connectivity issues during the maintenance.
